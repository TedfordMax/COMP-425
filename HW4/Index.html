Beginner trails are easiest with a gradual slope and no extra terrain, they can require a special chairlift.
Intermediate trails are more inverted than beginner trails but have no extra terrain, they are generally longer than beginners
Expert trails are EZ but more inverted than intermediate and also can have tricky terrain like trees/moguls.
